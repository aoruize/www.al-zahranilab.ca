Interested in Joining the Lab?

Postdoctoral Fellows

Please contact Khalid with your CV, three references, and a brief description of your research interests. We are particularly interested in candidates with a background in functional genomics.

Graduate Students

Graduate students can join through the Department of Molecular Genetics. Please check the departmental website for application procedures and deadlines. Informal inquiries are welcome — please email Khalid.
