Our Team

Principal Investigator

Khalid Al-Zahrani

Montrose, Scotland

Khalid is an Assistant Professor at the Donnelly Centre for Cellular and Biomolecular Research, University of Toronto. His research focuses on developing and applying functional genomics tools and mouse models to understand how large-scale chromosomal alterations drive tumourigenesis.
