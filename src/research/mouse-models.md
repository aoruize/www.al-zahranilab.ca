We aim to develop new mouse models of cancer and disease that are amenable to functional interrogation through direct <em>in vivo</em> genetic manipulation. Currently our lab focuses on identifying the earliest drivers of brain, breast, and ovarian cancers by genetically manipulating cells within their native environment.
